Tapovan International School

The school spread over 23 acres of prime land in Mahesana in lush green surroundings encapsulated with the beauty of naturally grown trees. It is a Day Boarding Cum Residential A.C School. Separate Boys and Girls A.C Hostel accommodation and centralized A.C buses too. The school is co-educational, affiliated to C.B.S.E. Particular emphasis is placed on the developmental of basic life skills, problem solving, time management, self control. T.I.S. nurtures children to the responsible adults and outstanding leaders who will make useful contribution to society and strengthen soul bounds with families and beyond.

Features

Library

A modern and appropriately staffed library is located in the academic complex.

In addition to a fully equipped library which includes thousands of books on various subjects/topics, the school subscribes to a large number of magazines and journals are available to ensure that students are up to date on current affairs, development in the field of science and technology.

The TIS School library has all what it takes to be a top notch. It has more than what a student will expect and to encourage reading habits among pupil, a wide range of books, magazines, journals are provided in the library.

  • Transport

    The School provides fully air-conditioned buses with GPRS installed in every bus for the safety and security of the students. This system helps in constant monitoring of all the school buses wherein the school authorities can track the current location of the buses and get real time updates of their movement. In case of any delay or breakdown – authorities can track the location of the bus and the required arrangements can be made at the earliest. Each bus is driven by a well-trained driver and has the presence of a care taker to escort the child.

In the hostel an experienced house masters assisted by caretakers provides care and affectionate personal attention to the needs of individual children. Active day schedule from wake-up call to bedtime. Modern kitchen and dining hall. Menu prepared by dieticians and food experts. Sumptuous and tasty meals, R.O. water and water coolers. Emphasis is on challenging the self on pursuing the limits of ability on not compromising with effort put in nor attention to detail.
